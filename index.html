Contrax - Fantasy Football Application

Project Synopsis

Contrax is a fantasy football application that combines the aspects of dynasty leagues with player contracts. Users take on the role of a GM, signing contracts with NFL players, paying them salaries, and managing their teams. Users can create and play in leagues that last several years, draft new rookies, bid and acquire talent as contracts expire, renew, or are acquired and transferred across teams.
